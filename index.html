About

Our history is our dignity

My passion for health and fitness was ignited when I was 16 years old and began training in the gym with my friends where we would spend hours each day after school. Pretty soon I was hooked on training and on a mission to become a bodybuilder- the famous physiques of Arnold Schwarzenegger and Ronnie Coleman inspired me to see how far I could push my own body transformation.

After completing cert 3 and 4, I began my personal training career in 2007 and since then I have helped hundreds of clients transform their lives through transforming their physiques and way of life.

I believe in using scientifically sound and evidence based methods to design and tailor both training programs and nutrition plans for each individual client.

With an ongoing love and passion for all aspects of training and a hunger to always learn more, I believe I can help anyone reach their health goals.

I have had the privilege to be a Vital strength sponsored athlete and work as a fitness model for reputable brands such as fitness first and fat blaster, as well as being featured in Cosmopolitan magazine.
